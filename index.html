Lin Zhang 张林

I am a second-year CS PhD in University of Wisconsin - Madison, supervised by Prof. Pedro Morgado .

Previously, I graduated from MSCV program in Carnegie Mellon University, supervised by Prof. Fernando de la Torre and Prof. Shayok Chakraborty. I received B.S. in CS and Math from Hong Kong University of Science and Technology with First Class Honors, supervised by Prof. Chi-Keung Tang and Prof. Yu-Wing Tai.

My research interests are computer vision and deep learning. Currently, I am interested in visual content generation conditioned on multiple modalities such as text and audio.
